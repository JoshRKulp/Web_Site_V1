Desk Headphone Setup



I wanted to upgrade my headphone setup at my desk at work. I like to listen to music at work via my iphone and get alerts from my desktop or play youtube videos. So I decide I did not want to keep moving my headphone cable back and forth between the two. Then I went to monoprice.com and got a “6inch 3.5mm Stereo Jack/Two 3.5mm Stereo Plug Cable ” to be able to get two inputs into one headphones.

I use the beats by dre as my headphones. Mostly because they look cool and work well with the iphone. Then I ran into a problem, I use the iphone cable below that has the mic and volume controals inline I was getting crazy skipping and volume changes with the iphone and no voices when the computer sound.

I ended up changing to the cable to the one with no mic and everything has been working fine.
